\chapter{DISCUSSION AND PROOFS}
\label{ch:experimental_result}

This chapter presents discussion and visual proof of the activities performed during the preparation of the Software Requirements Specification (SRS). Trello was used for task management and progress tracking, while Discord was used for communication, discussion, and file sharing.

These tools helped maintain organization, ensured timely completion of tasks, and supported proper coordination during the preparation of Section 1.5 (Risk Definition) and Section 2.5 (Assumptions).

\section{Trello Activity Proof}

Trello was used to manage and track tasks related to the SRS documentation. Tasks were organized into \textbf{To Do}, \textbf{Doing}, and \textbf{Done} categories to monitor progress and ensure accountability among team members.
