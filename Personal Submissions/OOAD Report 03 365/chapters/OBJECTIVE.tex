\chapter{OBJECTIVE}\label{obj}

The main objectives of this document are as follows:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item To establish a clear and structured Software Requirements Specification (SRS) for the proposed multi-tenant e-commerce system.
    \item To identify and define the intended users, stakeholders, and primary use cases of the platform.
    \item To analyze the overall product scope, operating environment, assumptions, and system constraints during the early stages of design.
    \item To document the functional requirements, user interactions, and system behaviors without focusing on implementation or technical design details.
    \item To ensure alignment between business objectives and system requirements through organized and comprehensive requirement analysis.
\end{enumerate}
