\chapter{CONCLUSIONS}\label{ch:conclusion}

This lab exercise successfully demonstrated the application of UML use case diagrams to model the requirements of the Multi-Tenant E-Commerce System. By creating both a high-level system diagram and a detailed vendor-focused diagram, we achieved the stated objectives of identifying actors, clarifying system scope, and elaborating on key functionalities like offer management and store customization.

The use of Lucidchart proved effective for creating professional, clear, and shareable UML diagrams. The \texttt{\textless{}\textless{}include\textgreater{}\textgreater{}} relationship identified in the offers diagram highlights how use case modeling can capture critical business logic early in the development lifecycle, preventing ambiguities during the coding phase.
