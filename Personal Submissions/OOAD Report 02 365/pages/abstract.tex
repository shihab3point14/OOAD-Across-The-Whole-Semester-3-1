This report presents the analysis and documentation of a Multi-Tenant E-Commerce System based on the Software Requirements Specification (SRS). The purpose of this work is to define the intended use, product scope, target users, and operating environment of the proposed system. The system is designed to support small and social-media-based businesses by providing a unified digital platform for online storefront management and wholesale product sourcing. Through requirement analysis, the report outlines how the system addresses operational inefficiencies, improves accessibility to digital commerce, and ensures scalability through a cloud-based, multi-tenant architecture.
