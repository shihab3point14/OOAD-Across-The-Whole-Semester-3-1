\chapter{OBJECTIVE}\label{obj}


The main objectives of this lab are:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item To understand the role of an SRS in defining software requirements clearly and systematically.
    \item To identify the intended users and use cases of a multi-tenant e-commerce platform.
    \item To analyze the product scope, operating environment, and system constraints at an early design stage.
    \item To document functional expectations and environmental requirements without focusing on implementation details.
    \item To ensure alignment between business goals and technical requirements through structured requirement analysis.
\end{enumerate}
