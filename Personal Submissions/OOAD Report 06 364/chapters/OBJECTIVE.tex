\chapter{OBJECTIVE}\label{obj}

The primary objectives of this study are:

\begin{itemize}
    \item To understand the fundamental concepts and notation of UML sequence diagrams.
    \item To analyze how sequence diagrams represent object interactions and message flows over time.
    \item To demonstrate the construction of sequence diagrams for common software scenarios.
    \item To evaluate the practical applications of sequence diagrams in software development lifecycle.
    \item To identify best practices for creating effective and maintainable sequence diagrams.
\end{itemize}
