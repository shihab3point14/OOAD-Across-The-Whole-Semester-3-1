This report provides a detailed review of five popular UML diagramming tools: Draw.io, SmartDraw, Lucidchart, PlantUML, and diagrams.net. Unified Modeling Language (UML) is widely used in software engineering to visually represent system designs before implementation. The report examines each tool’s features, advantages, and limitations, focusing on accessibility, ease of use, collaboration, and compatibility with different environments. Draw.io and diagrams.net are highlighted for their free availability and suitability for students, while SmartDraw and Lucidchart offer professional-grade features with cloud integration. PlantUML demonstrates the benefits of text-based, version-control-friendly diagrams. Based on the review, the report provides a comparison of the tools and justifies the selection of the most suitable UML tool for academic and small-team projects. The findings aim to help students and teams choose the right diagramming tool for efficient UML design and documentation.
