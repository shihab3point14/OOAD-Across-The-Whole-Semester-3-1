\section{Objective}

The main objective of this report is to review and compare five popular UML diagramming tools—Draw.io, SmartDraw, Lucidchart, PlantUML, and diagrams.net—focusing on their features, advantages, and limitations. The report aims to provide insights into each tool’s usability, accessibility, collaboration capabilities, and suitability for academic and small-team projects. By evaluating these tools, the report seeks to guide students and teams in selecting the most appropriate UML diagramming tool for efficient system modeling and documentation.
