\chapter{CONCLUSIONS}\label{ch:conclusion}


After reviewing and comparing the five UML diagramming tools—Draw.io, SmartDraw, Lucidchart, PlantUML, and diagrams.net—based on their features, advantages, and limitations, the team evaluated their suitability for academic and small-team projects. Each tool has its strengths: Draw.io and diagrams.net are free and beginner-friendly, SmartDraw offers professional features, and PlantUML is ideal for text-based diagrams and version control. 

Following thorough consideration and team discussion, we have chosen \textbf{Lucidchart} as the most suitable tool for our project. Its intuitive interface, real-time collaboration capabilities, cloud accessibility, and versatile template library make it ideal for efficiently creating and managing UML diagrams within our team.
