This laboratory activity focuses on exploring and evaluating different UML (Unified Modeling Language) drawing tools through collaborative teamwork. A group of team members was formed, and each member was assigned a specific UML tool to research and analyze, including Lucidchart, Draw.io, PlantUML, Diagrams.net, and SmartDraw. The objective of the experiment was to understand the features, advantages, and limitations of commonly used UML diagramming tools and to determine the most suitable tool for team-based project work. Each member prepared a brief review of their assigned tool, after which the team compared all tools based on usability, cost, collaboration features, and suitability for academic projects. The activity emphasized teamwork, communication, research skills, and decision-making in selecting appropriate software development tools. This exercise provides a foundational understanding of UML diagram creation and tool selection, which are essential for object-oriented analysis, system design, and effective project management in software development.
