\section{Objectives}

This lab aimed to:\\
• See how work moves step-by-step using columns on a board.\\
• Practice working together as a team and handing out jobs.\\
• Get better at doing more than one task at a time when possible.\\
• Check each other’s work, especially the calculations.\\
• Keep a full record using pictures and written notes.\\
• Look at how the team worked and figure out what could be done better next time.




\section{Expected Outcomes}
• Successful completion of all nine assigned tasks with documented evidence\\
• Identification of tasks suitable for parallel execution\\
• Implementation of a peer testing mechanism for quality assurance\\
• Understanding of team member roles and responsibilities\\
• Recognition of challenges in team communication and task clarity\\
• Development of insights for improving collaborative workflow management\\
