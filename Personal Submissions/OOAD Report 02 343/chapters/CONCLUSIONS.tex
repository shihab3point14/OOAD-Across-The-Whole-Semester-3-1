\chapter{CONCLUSIONS}\label{ch:conclusion}

This lab activity successfully documented the foundational requirements of a Multi-Tenant E-Commerce System by analyzing the SRS . The study clarified the system’s purpose, intended users, and operating environment, emphasizing its role in supporting small and social-media-driven businesses. By defining the product scope and environmental constraints early, the SRS helps reduce ambiguity and provides a shared understanding among stakeholders. This requirement-focused approach ensures that future design and development phases can proceed in a structured and aligned manner, increasing the likelihood of delivering a scalable, secure, and user-friendly software solution.
