I was responsible for writing Section 1.3 (Intended Use) and Section 2.3 (Operating Environment) of the Software Requirements Specification (SRS) for our group project titled “Multi-Tenant E-Commerce System.” My task involved defining how the system would be used by different stakeholders and describing the technical environment in which the system would operate. After completing the drafts, the sections were shared in the “srs-discussion” Discord channel for review. Based on group feedback, minor modifications were made, and the finalized content was integrated into SRS Chapters 1 and 2.
