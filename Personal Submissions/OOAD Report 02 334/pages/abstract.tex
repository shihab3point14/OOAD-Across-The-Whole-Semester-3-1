This lab report details the process of designing UML Sequence Diagrams for four distinct software system scenarios: a School Attendance System , an Exam Administration Process, a University Course Enrollment system , and an Online Shopping platform. The objective was to translate detailed textual requirements into dynamic visual models that map the chronological flow of messages between actors and system components. By utilizing PlantUML, lifelines and activation periods were accurately represented to model dynamic system behaviors. The resulting diagrams provide a clear blueprint of system interactions, demonstrating the practical application of sequence diagrams in capturing system requirements before the development phase begins.
