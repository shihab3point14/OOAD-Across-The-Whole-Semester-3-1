\chapter{OBSERVATIONS AND DISCUSSIONS}\label{ch:experimental_result}
The team successfully completed all nine tasks using the Kanban board. Tasks were visualized and tracked across four columns (To Do, Doing, Test, Done). Data collection and arithmetic tasks (like summing ages) were executed efficiently in parallel, while narrative tasks (like the travel distance) caused initial confusion. Peer verification was effective for calculations but highlighted data inconsistency, especially in the "units studied" task. The board clearly showed bottlenecks, such as when multiple sticky notes waited in the "Test" column. Team communication improved as members took on clear roles (e.g., mover, calculator, verifier).
