\chapter{OBJECTIVE}\label{obj}

The primary objectives of this laboratory activity were:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item To understand workflow visualization through column-based progress tracking
    \item To experience team coordination and task delegation in a controlled environment
    \item To practice parallel task execution where applicable
    \item To implement a peer verification (testing) process for calculation-based tasks
    \item To document the entire process through photographic evidence and notes
    \item To analyze team dynamics and identify process improvements
\end{enumerate}



\section{Expected Outcomes}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Successful completion of all nine assigned tasks with documented evidence
    \item Identification of tasks suitable for parallel execution
    \item Implementation of a peer testing mechanism for quality assurance
    \item Understanding of team member roles and responsibilities
    \item Recognition of challenges in team communication and task clarity
    \item Development of insights for improving collaborative workflow management
\end{itemize}
