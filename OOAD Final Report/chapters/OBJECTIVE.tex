\chapter{OVERVIEW}\label{obj}
The Multi-tenant E-commerce System is a modern and scalable platform designed to support multiple vendors operating independent online stores within a single, unified infrastructure. The system follows a multi-tenant architecture where each vendor is treated as a separate entity with isolated data storage, ensuring strong data security, privacy, and flexibility for customization.

The primary goal of this system is to provide a complete e-commerce solution that allows vendors to easily manage their products, customers, and transactions. Vendors are provided with a dedicated dashboard through which they can control inventory, manage product listings, create promotional offers, and customize their storefront appearance. This enables businesses of different sizes to efficiently launch and maintain their online presence.

The platform supports a seamless shopping experience for customers by integrating multiple payment methods such as online payment gateways and cash on delivery. It also includes order management features such as order placement, tracking, and delivery updates. Integration with third-party logistics services ensures real-time tracking and efficient product delivery.

In addition to core functionalities, the system offers advanced features such as analytics dashboards for monitoring sales and customer behavior, automated notification systems, and customer review management. Security is enhanced through mechanisms such as authentication and controlled access to vendor-specific data.

Overall, the Multi-tenant E-commerce System provides a robust, secure, and flexible environment that benefits both vendors and customers. It simplifies the process of managing multiple online stores while maintaining high performance, scalability, and user satisfaction.
